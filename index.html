Our Story

Jainco Lifestlye - Quality Tiles & Home Building Materials

Jainco Lifestyle is Bokakhat's most trusted one-stop home building and décor store, located on Assam Trunk Road opposite the Block Office. We offer an extensive collection of premium quality tiles, sanitaryware, bath fittings, shower enclosures, electricals, plywood, hardware, and much more — everything you need to build, renovate, or beautify your home without having to visit multiple stores. Our curated selection blends modern design with lasting durability, catering to every budget and taste.

With a proud 5-star rating from our customers, we at Jainco Lifestyle believe that every home deserves the best. Whether you are tiling a single room or furnishing an entire house, our knowledgeable staff is here to guide you through our vast range of branded materials and help you find the perfect fit. We also offer special bulk pricing and deals for large projects, making us the preferred choice for homeowners, contractors, and interior designers across the Golaghat district and beyond.
